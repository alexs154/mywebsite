Peppermill Design

Overview

For this project I was contracted to reverse engineer an existing collection of peppermills. I created detailed CAD models of five different mills, and then created technical drawings for manufacturing. Through working with a factory in China, I was able to learn about the manufacturing process and tolerances needed for mass production. We are currently in the process of manufacturing 10,000 units for sale in the US market.

Skilled Used

Fabrication, Machining, Torque and Precision Measurments, Problem Solving and Diagnostics, SolidWorks
