Honda CL450 Rebuild

Overview

For this project I came across a 1970 Honda CL450 for a price I couldn't turn down. The bike was sitting in a field for the past 50 years, so it needed everything from a complete engine rebuild to completely repainting the frame. Through this project I was able to learn every feature about how this motorcycle works, and after was able to model the engine in Solidworks as a bonues project.

Mechanical Skilled Used

Fabrication, Machining, Torque and Precision Measurments, Problem Solving and Diagnostics, SolidWorks
